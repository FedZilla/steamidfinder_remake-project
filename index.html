How to find your Steam ID

Finding your SteamID is easy. All you need to do is log into Steam via your web browser or the Steam application. From there, click your username in the top-right corner of the window and select ‘Account details’ from the dropdown menu. Your 17 digit SteamID will appear near the top of this screen, right below your Steam username. These numbers are known as your Steam 64-bit ID, and they act as a unique identifier for your Steam account. Copy them, and you’re ready to go.

How to find your Steam Hex

Once you’ve followed the steps above to find your SteamID, you can use our tool to convert it into a Steam Hex. Simply copy the 17 digit SteamID into the search bar, and we’ll convert it into a Steam Hex ID in a matter of seconds.

  • SteamID64 - 76561197960287930
  • CustomURL - gabelogannewell
  • Full Steam URL - https://steamcommunity.com/profiles/76561197960287930
